COVID-19 Prevention

How to prevent yourself from catching COVID-19?

During these days, COVID-19 is still roaming the lands of the Earth. Even though the chances of getting infected are lower, we must still follow some tips that might help avoid ourselves from catching the said sickness.

Some useful pieces of advices are the following:

  • Wear your masks all the time when you're outdoors.
  • Make sure to have good ventilation when indoors.
  • Maintain your distance from others, at least 1 meter apart.
  • Avoid touching surfaces in public areas and especially in health facilities.
  • Get yourself vaccinated to protect not only yourself but also the whole community.

How to properly wash your hands?

Keeping our hands clean is important all the time. We can maintain proper hygiene. Viruses can get into the body through the eyes, mouth, and nose. People, from time to time, touch their faces. If we let our hands become dirty, there is a chance the virus can enter our bodies.

Here are the following steps to keep our hands spotless:

  1. Wet your hands and apply soap to cover all surfaces of the hands; let the water run smoothly to avoid touching the tap later on.
  2. Rub your hand's palm to your palm to obtain a good quantity of foam.
  3. Rub your right palm over the back of the left hand with interlaced fingers and vice versa.
  4. Rub palm to palm again with fingers interlaced.
  5. Rub the back of your fingers to opposing palms with fingers interlocked; repeat this action for each hand.
  6. Rub rotationally left thumb clasped in the right palm and vice versa.
  7. To clean the tips of the fingers, rub rotationally backward and forwards with clasped fingers of the right hand in the left palm and vice versa.
  8. Rinse your hands thoroughly with running water.
  9. Dry your hands thoroughly with a single-use towel.
  10. If the tap is not elbow operated, use the towel to turn off the tap without touching it directly with your hands.
  11. Your hands are now clean.
